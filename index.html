About Us

The Bengali Cultural Association of India in Spain, a non-profit organisation founded in 2021, is passionately committed to enriching the lives of our vibrant subcontinent community. Our mission is to foster unity and establish a charity fund to support the educational and foundational needs of children from the subcontinent.

In 2025, we will proudly celebrate our third year of Durga Puja and host a series of cultural events including Saraswati Puja, Heart-2-Heart concert, Shakti performance, and Hindi Divas! With support from the Embassy of India and the consulate in Barcelona, our mission grows stronger every year.
